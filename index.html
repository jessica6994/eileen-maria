Katherine Ann Cumberland

Lead Designer and Haute Couture Specialst (BA, MA)

Katherine has emmersed herself in the world of bridal design from a very young age and she has devoted her life to becoming a respected haute courture specialist. After working within many bridal projects she went on to university to complete a BA in Fashion production and marketing. This gave her many opportunities and contacts in the fashion industry however, she knew she still had a lot to learn. Katherine took a leap of faith and gave up everything to move to Paris to attend complete her MA in Haute Couture from the prestigious Paris College of Art, following in the footsteps of many inspirational designers such as Tom Ford.


Today, she is dedicated to creating beautiful bespoke gowns for brides across the nation.
